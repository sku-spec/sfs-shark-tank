📋 Rules

Everything you need to know to play

🧠
Build Your Product
  • You have 15 minutes to invent a fake product
  • Must include: name, what it does, price point, and tagline
  • The more absurd, the better
  • Use anything in the room as a prop
🎤
The Pitch
  • 2 min — pitch to the Sharks
  • 5 min — Sharks ask questions
  • 3 min — Sharks score
  • All team members must participate
🦈
Scoring
  • Each Shark scores 1–5 on three categories
  • Creativity — how original is the idea?
  • Pitch Energy — did they sell it?
  • Would You Invest? — gut feel
  • Max score per team: 45 points
⚡
Facilitator Powers
  • Can add a silly constraint at any time
  • Can extend or shorten any phase
  • Can veto a score if too generous or too harsh
  • Final ruling on all disputes
💡 Prompts If Teams Get Stuck
  • "A subscription service for..."
  • "An app that helps executives..."
  • "A device that solves the problem of..."
  • "A fintech product that finally fixes..."
  • "A Square product for people who..."
⚡ Silly Constraints (Use If They're Too Serious)
  • Your product must be pink and solve a problem only left-handed people have
  • Your entire pitch must be delivered as a rap
  • Your product name must be a pun
  • You must use at least one object from the room as a demo
